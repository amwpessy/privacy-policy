English

AURUM Gold ("the App") respects and protects user privacy.

We do not collect any personal information. The App:

  • Requires no sign-up or login and collects no name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information;
  • Does not collect location data;
  • Contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs and does not track users;
  • Stores your watchlist, price alerts, and region settings locally on your device only; nothing is uploaded to any server.

Network requests: The App only requests prices from gold-api.com and exchange rates from exchangerate-api.com. These requests contain no information that identifies you.

Push notifications: The App uses local iOS notifications to alert you when a price target is reached. Notifications are generated on your device and do not pass through any remote server.

Disclaimer: Prices are for reference only and do not constitute investment advice.
