01

Information We Collect

XiYing Crypto does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, and we never ask for your name, email address, location, or any other identifying information.

No personal data is collected at any point while using this app.
02

Watchlist & Local Storage

Your custom watchlist (coins you star and save) is stored exclusively on your own device using standard iOS local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to our servers (we have none)
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Can be removed at any time by deleting the app
03

Third-Party Data Sources

The app fetches public market data from third-party APIs, including CoinGecko. These network requests contain no personal information about you — only standard query parameters to retrieve public price data. Please refer to CoinGecko's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle requests.

04

Analytics & Advertising

XiYing Crypto contains no third-party analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, advertising networks, or behavioral tracking of any kind. We do not use Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or any similar service.

The app is completely ad-free and tracking-free.
05

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from anyone, we do not knowingly collect data from children either.

06

Data Security

All network requests made by this app use HTTPS encryption. Since we collect no personal data and operate no servers storing user information, there is no user data at risk of breach.

07

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
